How it works

A quick guide to the method behind the numbers.

The core formula

Social value (£) = residents with the outcome × proxy value (£) × net adjustment, summed across outcomes, for each supplier — calculated separately for committed and delivered figures.

Committed vs. delivered

For each outcome you record what the supplier committed to at bid/contract stage, and what they have actually delivered so far. The tool shows a delivery rate and a status (Complete / On track / In progress / Behind) per supplier, so the leaderboard reflects real performance, not just promises. Add a note or link in the evidence field for each outcome so claims are auditable.

Where the values come from

Defaults follow HACT's UK Social Value Bank approach (Wellbeing Valuation / WELLBY, HM Treasury approved). The figures shipped here are illustrative placeholders — the official current values require a HACT licence and should be entered on “Outcome values”.

The net adjustment

Per supplier, set a net adjustment (0–100%) to discount for deadweight, attribution and drop‑off, in line with SROI/HACT practice. Leave at 100% for gross value.

Value for money

Enter a contract value to see a Social Value Ratio (£ of social value per £1 of contract), so a small supplier can be compared fairly with a large one.
